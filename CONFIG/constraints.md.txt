ORION – System Constraints

Time Constraints:
- Exams are not active.
- Daily ORION work limit: 1–2 hours max.
- Study time cannot be reduced for earning tasks.

Scope Constraints:
- Phase 1 only.
- No automation.
- No external tools unless explicitly allowed.

Behavior Constraints:
- No multitasking.
- No expanding features mid-week.
- No system redesign during execution days.

Earning Constraints:
- Only low-risk, ethical methods.
- No distraction-heavy platforms.
- Earnings must not interfere with exams.

Override Rule:
If any conflict occurs, academics > health > system > money.
